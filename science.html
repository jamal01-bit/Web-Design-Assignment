The Science of Global Warming

The greenhouse effect is basically a natural process that warms the Earth's surface so that it's possible for life to exist here. When the Sun's energy reaches our atmosphere, some of it is reflected back into space and the rest is absorbed and re-radiated by these greenhouse gases.

I researched that the key greenhouse gases are:

  • Carbon Dioxide (CO2)
  • Methane (CH4)
  • Nitrous Oxide (N2O)
  • Water Vapor (H2O)

How human activities have changed things

While the greenhouse effect is natural, we have increased the amount of greenhouse gases by a lot. This is mostly because of the burning of fossil fuels (coal, oil, gas), cutting down forests which means there are fewer trees to absorb CO2, and the emissions from industrial processes and farming.
