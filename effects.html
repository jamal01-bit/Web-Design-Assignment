Climate Change Effects

Global Environmental Impacts

The climate change is causing radical changes in our environment of which most of them are already being realized and will continue to grow.

  • Rising Sea Levels: With the melting of ice sheets and glaciers and the warming of sea water, the sea level rises and endangers the population of the coastal areas.
  • Extreme Weather: Floods,droughts, wild fires and tornados are becoming regular and worsening in many parts of the planet.
  • Changing Ecosystems: There are several changing plants and animals that are struggling to adapt to the changing weather patterns and temperatures.

Future Risks

If global warming exceeds 1.5°C above pre-industrial levels, the risks of catastrophic impacts on our environment and societies will significantly increase.
