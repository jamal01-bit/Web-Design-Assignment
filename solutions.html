Climate Solutions

Renewable Energy

The move from fossil fuels to renewable energy will be the most effective way to reduce greenhouse gas emissions

  • Solar Power: Capturing energy from the sun using solar panels.
  • Wind Power: Using wind turbines to generate electricity.
  • Hydroelectric Power: Using the flow of water to generate electricity.

Comparison: Fossil Fuels vs Renewable Energy

This table compares the long term effects of our current energy sources compared to renewable alternatives.

Energy Source Type Carbon Emissions Sustainability,
Coal and Oil Fossil Fuel Very High Non Renewable
Solar and Wind Renewable Zero Infinite
Natural Gases Fossil Fuel High Limited Supply

Energy Efficiency

By improving energy efficiency in our transport, buildings and sources of power; we can reduce our carbon footprint significantly.
Some ways we can do this is via LED lights, insulation and using more energy efficient appliances.
