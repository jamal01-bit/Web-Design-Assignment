Renewable Energy

Transitioning from fossil fuels to renewable energy sources is one of the most effective ways to reduce greenhouse gas emissions.

  • Solar Power: Capturing energy from the sun using photovoltaic cells.
  • Wind Power: Using wind turbines to generate electricity.
  • Hydroelectric Power: Generating electricity from the flow of water.
  • Geothermal Energy: Using heat from the Earth's interior to produce power.

Energy Efficiency

Improving energy efficiency in our buildings, transport, and industry can also significantly reduce our carbon footprint. This includes using LED lighting, improving insulation, and choosing more efficient appliances.
