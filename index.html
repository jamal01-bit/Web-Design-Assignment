The Climate Crisis

My Website on Climate Change

My Web Design and Development unit web site. I decided to write my project on climate change since it is one of the things that I believe everyone should be concerned about.about. Climate change is described as the general changes in weather patterns and temperatures over a long period of time over the earth.
These changes have been natural since the 1800s, the primary cause of the issue has been human activities. This is largely due to the combustion of fossil fuels (such as coal, oil and gas) that generate heat retaining gases. By the time you've finished browsing through this website, you will be able to understand why we should act now.

Main issues I cover

  • Global Temperature Rise
  • Oceans Warming Up
  • Glacial Meltdown

Why It Matters

Climate change is already being experienced all over the world.Extreme weather conditions such as hurricanes and droughts, as well as the loss of biodiversity, are serious and need to be addressed on a global scale. From extreme weather events like hurricanes and droughts to the loss of biodiversity, the consequences are profound and require urgent global action.
